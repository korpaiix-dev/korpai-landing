2026-06-11 and change often — ALWAYS verify on the official pricing page before

| LINE OA Free plan | keyword auto-reply, 1:1 chat replies | broadcast ~300 msgs/mo | paid plan ~1,200 THB/mo; overage ~0.06–0.10 THB/msg |

| n8n self-host + LLM API | unlimited workflows | flow complexity / maintenance | VPS + tokens + learning curve |

## The 4 hidden costs of every "free" stack

1. Channel quota overages (broadcast/push messages)
2. LLM tokens — Thai text costs 2–3x English for the same sentence
3. Your own hours: 10–20 h setup + 2–5 h/week maintenance
4. Risk: no guardrails, no handoff, no PDPA log

True monthly cost = overages + tokens + (hours x hourly wage) + risk.
When that exceeds a managed plan, free is your most expensive option.
